Blackjack Rules

Everything you need to know to play

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand total closer to 21 without going over (busting). You are not competing against other players — only against the dealer.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) — Worth their face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) — Worth 10
  • Aces — Worth 1 or 11, whichever benefits your hand more. A hand with an Ace counted as 11 is called a "soft" hand

How a Hand is Played

  1. Place your bet before cards are dealt
  2. You and the dealer each receive two cards. Both of your cards are face up; the dealer has one face up and one face down (the "hole card")
  3. You decide how to play your hand (see actions below)
  4. Once you finish, the dealer reveals their hole card and plays according to fixed rules
  5. Hands are compared and bets are settled

Player Actions

Hit

Take another card. You can hit as many times as you want, but if your total exceeds 21 you bust and lose immediately.

Stand

Keep your current hand. No more cards are drawn and play passes to the dealer.

Double Down

Double your bet and receive exactly one more card. This is a powerful move when you have a strong starting position.

Split

If your first two cards have the same rank, you can split them into two separate hands, each with its own bet. Each hand is then played independently. In this version, you can split up to 3 times (4 hands total). Split Aces receive only one additional card each.

Dealer Rules

  • The dealer must hit on any total of 16 or below
  • The dealer must hit on a soft 17 (a hand totaling 17 that includes an Ace counted as 11) — this is the "H17" rule used in this version
  • The dealer must stand on hard 17 or higher
  • The dealer has no choice — these rules are fixed

Winning and Payouts

  • Blackjack — An Ace + 10-value card on the initial deal. Typically pays 3:2 (1.5x your bet)
  • Regular Win — Your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, or the dealer busts. Pays 1:1 (even money)
  • Push — You and the dealer have the same total. Your bet is returned
  • Loss — The dealer's hand is closer to 21, or you busted. You lose your bet

Game Configuration in This Version

  • 6-deck shoe
  • Dealer hits soft 17 (H17)
  • Double after split is allowed
  • Split up to 3 times (4 hands max)
  • Split Aces receive one card only
  • Re-splitting Aces is not allowed
